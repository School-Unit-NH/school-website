Standard ICT Suite

At EaglesFlight School, our Standard ICT Suite boasts a world-class laboratory, featuring modern computers, 3D Projection Television, and Internet facilities, facilitating student development in computer skills and knowledge. Embracing a Do-It-Yourself (DIY) approach,

We aim to cultivate communication, personal enrichment, efficiency, and collaboration. Each day in our ICT suite presents exciting opportunities for learning and growth.

Science Lab

At EaglesFlight School, our state-of-the-art Science Laboratory fosters hands-on learning crucial for understanding complex scientific concepts. Through engaging experiments and interactive demonstrations, students explore physics, chemistry, biology, and more.

They manipulate materials, observe reactions, and draw connections between theory and practice. Our laboratory equip students with critical thinking skills and problem-solving abilities essential for success in today's dynamic world.

Beautiful Creche

Welcome to our Beautiful Creche, where every detail is designed to provide a nurturing haven for our youngest learners. With a focus on safety, comfort, and stimulating environment.

We offer a space where children can explore, learn, and grow under the care of experienced educators. Join us in shaping bright futures from the very beginning in capable and competent hands.

State of the Art Library

Step into our State of the Art Library, a treasure trove of knowledge and imagination. With a vast collection of books, resources, and digital materials, it's a sanctuary where students delve into the world of literature, research, and exploration.

Explore, discover, and expand horizons within the pages of our library's diverse offerings, where every story whispers the promise of new adventures.
