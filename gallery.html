Music Classes
Age:
4 - 11 Years
Time:
9 - 1PM
Capacity:
30 Pupils

Engagement with music enhances children’s cognitive abilities, creativity, and emotions. The EaglesFlight School Orchestra and Band is a multidimensional performing arts group dedicated to nurturing young talents in music. Their mission encompasses three vital objectives:

  • Skillful Performance: Fostering musical excellence both collectively and individually among children.
  • Self-Expression: Providing a creative platform for every child to express themselves through music.
  • Parental Fulfillment: Encouraging parents to discover fulfillment within their children’s musical journey.
Their harmonious endeavours span across a diverse array of musical instruments, igniting melodies that resonate with promise and passion.

Ballet Classes
Age:
4 - 11 Years
Time:
9-1 PM
Capacity:
30 Pupils

Our Ballet Classes: A harmonious blend of grace and discipline, our ballet program is a sanctuary for aspiring dancers. Led by seasoned professionals, we nurture young talents, guiding them through the delicate pirouettes of technique and the lyrical leaps of expression.

From pliés at the barre to grand jetés across the studio, our students learn the language of movement. Whether they dream of the spotlight or seek solace in the studio’s embrace, each step is a brushstroke on their artistic canvas.

We encourage every dancer to find their unique voice within the classical notes. The stage becomes their confidence. Our ballet classes become a shared journey, Join us, where pointed shoes meet dreams, and the echo of steps lingers in the air.

Wordsmith Club
Age:
4 - 11 Years
Time:
9-1 PM
Capacity:
30 Pupils

Wordsmith Club fosters a lifelong love for learning and reading through a hybrid teaching approach. Using diverse aids like building blocks, puzzles, and creative writing, we ignite pupils’ eagerness to read, enhance vocabulary, and nurture expressive communication.

Our age-appropriate fiction and non-fiction books cultivate frequent reading habits. In our non-traditional, friendly environment, pupils explore genres, develop critical skills, and build cultural awareness. We celebrate fluency, literature, and create a home for young writers and readers.
Impact: With the introduction of the Wordsmith Club, Love of literature has been greatly promoted.
